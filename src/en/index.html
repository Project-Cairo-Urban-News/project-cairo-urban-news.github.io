Welcome to the News of Cairo!

On this site, you can access a textual database of selected Arabic (and some Ottoman Turkish) articles about Cairo's urban development from al-Waqāʾiʿal-Miṣriyyah, the official Egyptian government journal, between 1828 and 1914. You can use this dataset in three ways:

  • 1) Search using the searchbar below.
  • 2) Check the index of organizations, roles, locations.
  • 3) Check the timeline page and click according to year.
