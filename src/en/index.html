--- Digital Cairo ---

Welcome to the News of Cairo!

On this site, you can access a textual database of selected Arabic (and some Ottoman Turkish) articles about Cairo's urban development from al-Waqāʾiʿ al-Miṣriyyah, the official Egyptian government journal, between 1828 and 1914. You can use this dataset in three ways:

1) search in the searchbox

2) check the index of organizations, roles, locations

3) check the timeline page and click according to year.
